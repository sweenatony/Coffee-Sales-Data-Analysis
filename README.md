# Coffee-Sales-Data-Analysis

## Project Overview

This project is focused on analyzing the coffee sales data. This project aims to find data-driven solutions to improve coffee business by generating actionable insights from the data by understanding the sales and product performance, customer preference by coffee type, customer behaviour and purchasing patterns, and coffee shopping trends over the time.

## Tools & Technologies

- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- MS Excel
- Power BI

## Dataset Overview

Coffee sales data from March 2024 – July 2024 containing:
- date	(The date on which the transaction occurred)
- datetime	(The exact timestamp (date & time) when the coffee was purchased)
- cash_type	 (Payment mode used for the transaction – card or cash)
- card	 (Unique customer ID for card users)
- money	 (Total amount spent in the transaction)
- coffee_name	 (Name of the coffee product purchased)

## Key Analysis Performed

- Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Sales trends over time
- Revenue trends over time
- Sales and Revenue by coffee types
- Payment type preferred by customers
- Popular coffee type preference by customers
- Coffee type choice of frequent customers

## Key Insights

- Most popular products: Americano with Milk, Latte, Cappuccino
- Peak sales hours: 10 AM to 7 PM
- Highest sales days: Tuesday to Thursday
- Card payments dominate (92%)
- A small group of loyal customers generates high revenue

## Business Recommendations

- Promote combo offers during peak hours
- Run discounts on Sunday & Monday
- Bundle low-selling items with bestsellers
- Introduce loyalty programs for repeat customers

## Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard is included to visualize key insights.
